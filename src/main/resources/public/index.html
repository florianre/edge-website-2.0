Club Overview

Sea paddles, white water, club sessions, and weekends away.

Edge Progressive Paddling is a dummy club homepage for now, but the direction is a real mixed-discipline kayaking club. Think sea kayaking on exposed coastlines, moving-water training, rescue practice, club trips, and the occasional waterfall obsession.

We welcome complete beginners, improvers, expedition dreamers, and people who own too many dry bags. The real copy can go here later; for now this page is just the themed public landing page.
